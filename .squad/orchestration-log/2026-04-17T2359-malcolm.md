## Key Design Decisions

1. **Macro Dominance (35%)** — ETF performance heavily driven by macro events (rate cycles, geopolitics, sector rotation)

2. **Longer Half-Lives** — Macro signals 18d vs 7d for stocks; sentiment 7d vs 3d; reflects slower ETF portfolio turnover

3. **Sector Window Cascade** — 40/120/200d lookback captures:
   - 40d: Recent sector momentum (tactical shifts)
   - 120d: Medium-term rotation (seasonal patterns)
   - 200d: Structural trend (bull/bear markets)

4. **Consensus Amplification** — Multi-source agreement boosts confidence; single outlier articles discounted

5. **Reduced Volatility Penalty** — 6× vs 10×: ETFs absorb volatility naturally; penalty too harsh causes whipsaws
