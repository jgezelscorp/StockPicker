### Key Contributions

#### Strategy Framework
- **Momentum/News Trader** (25% capital, 5-min reactive cadence)
  - Target: Short-term dislocations from breaking news and social buzz
  - Capital allocation: 25% target, 10-35% range, 20% cash buffer
  - Risk limits: 8 positions max, 15% per position, -5% stop loss, -3% trailing stop

- **Value/Contrarian Investor** (35% capital, 4-hour cadence)
  - Target: Fundamentally undervalued stocks where market overreacted
  - Capital allocation: 35% target, 20-45% range, 10% cash buffer
  - Risk limits: 12 positions max, 12% per position, -12% stop loss (no trailing)

- **Macro ETF Strategist** (40% capital, daily to monthly cadence)
  - Target: Top-down allocation based on economic regime
  - Capital allocation: 40% target, 25-50% range, 5% cash buffer
  - Risk limits: 6 positions max, 10% per position, 2% max rebalance per cycle

#### Signal Pipeline Redesign

**Momentum Pipeline (5 signals):**
- News Velocity (30%) — headlines-per-hour acceleration + urgency keywords
- Social Buzz (25%) — Reddit + StockTwits mention acceleration + sentiment
- Volume Spike (20%) — institutional vs retail volume patterns
- Price Momentum (15%) — 5/10-day returns, RSI overbought/oversold
- Search Acceleration (10%) — Google Trends velocity

**Value Pipeline (5 signals):**
- Fundamental Value (35%) — P/E, PEG, EV/EBITDA, FCF yield, analyst targets
- Contrarian Sentiment (20%) — Inverted logic: buy when everyone sells + fundamentals agree
- Mean Reversion (20%) — Price vs 200-day mean + Bollinger Bands + 52-week position
- Insider Activity (15%) — SEC EDGAR Form 4 filings, buy/sell clustering
- Quality Score (10%) — ROE, D/E, profit margin, revenue growth, FCF

**Macro ETF Pipeline (5 signals):**
- Macro Regime (30%) — FRED data: CPI, unemployment, PMI, fed funds, yield curve → growth/slowdown/recession/recovery/stagflation
- Yield Curve (20%) — 2Y/10Y Treasury spread, inversion detection
- Sector Rotation (20%) — Relative performance of 11 sector ETFs vs S&P 500
- Political Risk (15%) — Election/regulation/tariff risk via GDELT + news
- ETF Relative Value (15%) — Expense ratio, tracking error, AUM, liquidity

#### Data Source Upgrades (7 new sources)
1. **FRED** — Macro indicators (CPI, unemployment, GDP, PMI, fed funds, yield curve, initial claims)
2. **Reddit API** — r/wallstreetbets, r/stocks, r/investing mention/engagement/buzz tracking
3. **SEC EDGAR** — Form 4 insider buy/sell filings
4. **StockTwits** — Real-time trader sentiment (fallback to Reddit)
5. **GDELT** — Geopolitical event tracking for political risk signal
6. **NewsAPI** — Fallback to Finnhub for broader news coverage
7. **Alpha Vantage** — Fallback to Yahoo for fundamentals and intraday data

#### Risk Management Architecture
- **Cross-strategy guardrails:** 5% drift triggers soft rebalance, 10% triggers hard trim
- **Correlation guard:** Momentum + Value same sector >40% flags manual review
- **Drawdown circuit breaker:** Portfolio -15% from peak pauses all entries 24h
- **Strategy-specific limits:** Different position counts, sector concentration, stop losses per strategy type
- **Total portfolio reserve:** Never below 10% cash

3. **Phase 3 (Week 3):** Create 5 new signal modules (macro ETF), integrate FRED/GDELT APIs
4. **Phase 4 (Week 4):** Scheduler redesign for per-strategy cadences, capital allocation enforcement, cross-strategy rebalancing logic
